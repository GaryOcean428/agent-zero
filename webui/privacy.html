Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Gary-Zero is an open-source AI agent framework that runs locally on your machine. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about data handling.

1.1 Local Data Storage

  • Chat History: Your conversations with Gary-Zero are stored locally on your machine in the application's memory and logs directories.
  • Configuration Settings: Your preferences and settings are stored locally in configuration files.
  • Files and Documents: Any files you upload or create are stored locally in your designated work directories.
  • Temporary Data: The application may create temporary files during operation, stored locally.

1.2 External Service Integration

When you configure Gary-Zero to use external services, data may be transmitted to those services according to their respective privacy policies:

  • AI Model Providers: OpenAI, Anthropic, or other configured AI services
  • Search Services: When using web search capabilities
  • Cloud Storage: If you configure cloud storage integrations
  • MCP Servers: External Model Context Protocol servers you connect to

2. How We Use Your Information

Gary-Zero processes your information to:

  • Provide AI agent assistance and task completion
  • Maintain conversation context and memory
  • Execute commands and code as requested
  • Interface with external services you configure
  • Improve your user experience through personalization

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Gary-Zero does not share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • External Services: When you explicitly configure integrations with external AI services, APIs, or tools
  • User-Initiated Actions: When you specifically request the agent to interact with external services
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage: Data is stored locally on your machine with standard file system permissions
  • Authentication: Optional basic authentication for web UI access
  • Transport Security: HTTPS support for secure communication
  • Access Control: Role-based access controls where applicable

5. Data Retention

  • Chat History: Retained until manually deleted by you
  • Log Files: Rotated based on your configuration settings
  • Temporary Files: Automatically cleaned up during normal operation
  • Configuration: Retained until manually changed or application is uninstalled

6. Your Rights and Choices

As a local application, you have full control over your data:

  • Access: All data is stored locally and accessible to you
  • Deletion: You can delete any data by removing files or using application features
  • Portability: Your data is stored in standard formats and can be exported
  • Control: You control all external service integrations and data sharing

7. Children's Privacy

Gary-Zero is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. International Data Transfers

Data processing occurs locally on your machine. Any data transmitted to external services depends on your configuration and the geographic location of those services.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the application and on our documentation.

10. Contact Information

For questions about this privacy policy or data practices:

  • Open an issue on our GitHub repository
  • Review our open-source documentation

11. Open Source Transparency

Gary-Zero is open-source software. You can review our code, data handling practices, and security implementations at https://github.com/GaryOcean428/gary-zero.

This privacy policy applies to the Gary-Zero application. External services integrated with Gary-Zero have their own privacy policies that govern data handled by those services.
